Interests

If I had to pick one, I'd say the broadest topic that encapsulates my research interests is "motion". How do things (objects, people, fluid bubbles, sound waves, etc.) move? Why do they move the way they do? How can we simulate/predict their movement (efficiently)?

Technically speaking, this means my research focuses on methods for physical simulation and physically-based animation. I aim to make it easier and more efficient to simulate various phenomena, and also to make it easier to create physically realistic animations.
