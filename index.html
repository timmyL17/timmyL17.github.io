About me

I'm currently a Ph.D. student in the Cornell Computer Science department Graphics Lab where I'm advised by Doug James.

My research interests include physically-based animation and physical simulation. I aim to make it easier and more efficient to simulate various phenomena, and also to make it easier to create physically realistic animations. My research is funded by a National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellowship.
